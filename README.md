## Features

- **AES-256 encryption**  
  Locks traffic down on hostile Wi‑Fi and untrusted networks.

- **No-Logs policy**  
  The point is privacy. “We don’t store what you do” isn’t optional.

- **Kill Switch**  
  If the tunnel drops, traffic stops. No silent leaks.

- **Global Servers**  
  More locations means less congestion and better routing options.

- **High speed**  
  Fast enough for streaming, calls, and large downloads without babysitting it.

- **Stable connection**  
  Doesn’t randomly fall apart when you switch networks or sleep/wake a laptop.

## FAQ

**1) Why use secure vpn instead of a free VPN?**  
Free VPNs often cut corners: limited servers, weaker controls, and murky logging. Secure VPN is built around privacy basics (AES-256, No-Logs, Kill Switch).

**2) Does the Kill Switch matter if I’m “just browsing”?**  
Yes. The most common leak is a brief disconnect. A Kill Switch prevents traffic from falling back to your normal connection.

**3) Will a VPN slow my internet down?**  
Any VPN adds overhead. Secure VPN focuses on **high speed** and a **stable connection** by using **Global Servers** and sensible routing.

**4) What should I check before connecting?**  
Enable **Kill Switch**, pick a nearby server for speed, and confirm you’re connected before logging into sensitive accounts.
